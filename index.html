About the project

Many of us spend hours hunched over desks or phones, slowly straining our backs and necks without noticing. The Connected Spine is a wearable system that gently watches your posture in real time.

Small sensors worn along the back - at the neck, upper back, and waist - detect when you slouch or lean too far. When your posture drifts beyond a set range, the device responds with a soft buzz at the area that needs attention, reminding you to adjust your posture. It is a quiet, personal nudge toward better posture awareness throughout the day.

Type
Wearable posture monitor
Sensing points
Neck - Upper Back - Waist
Feedback
Targeted haptic buzz
